About Moody Food

You are what you eat, so the saying goes. What if you wanted to create a meal based on your mood? Better yet, how about a meal that can improve your mood?


Moody Food was created to give recipe recommendations based on ingredients that are scientifically proven to alter mood. What better satisfaction than creating your own meal, not to mention the added health benefits. The hopes are that people make healthier food choices and adopt a healthy lifestyle altogether.


Illustrations were created by artist Desiree Marin.
